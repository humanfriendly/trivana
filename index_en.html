                        Conditions

                        •  Services provided by appointment only.
                        •  For home cleanings, sufficient space to circulate around the vehicle is required. To ensure an optimal result, a covered area sheltered from drafts is ideal. Moreover, water and electricity must be easily accessible and within a radius of 10 meters.
                        •  Payments are to be be made cash on delivery of the cleaned vehicle. Invoices will be sent by post or email.
                        •  Vehicles that are unusually dirty will be subject to an extra fee (customer is always notified beforehand).
                        •  A surcharge of €0,30/km must be paid for travel that is outside of a 10km radius.
